# Assignment 2: Age and Gender Distortion in Online Media and AI

## Overview
This assignment was completed for the Practical Data Science subject during my MSc studies.

The assignment was based on a Nature article about how online media can distort age and gender perceptions, and how artificial intelligence models can reproduce these biases.

## Source Paper
The assignment was based on the article:

**Online media distorts age and gender perceptions**  
Ana Macanovic, Nature, 2025.

The article discusses research by Guilbeault et al. on how online content represents men as older than women in the same occupations and social roles, and how this pattern is also reflected in AI systems.

## Project Purpose
The purpose of this assignment was to explore how data science can be used to study social bias in online content and artificial intelligence.

## Key Ideas from the Paper
The paper explains that online images and text can create distorted representations of social reality. In particular, women are often shown as younger than men in the same occupations or social roles.

The article also explains that this bias can be reproduced by AI models. For example, the paper describes how AI systems associate older age more strongly with men and younger age more strongly with women.
